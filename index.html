Hi there and welcome to my portfolio site! I am a multi-passionate designer with many years of hands-on experience in digital design and web design. My unique blend of design and coding skills allows me to create designs that are unique, professional, and user-friendly.

About Me

I'm a Clevelander, born and raised, who loves creating and learning new things. Tinkering with technology always fascinated me, which is why I began specializing in designing for the "online world" a few years ago. My design style is more along the lines of "simple yet fun". I've learned from experience that the best designs that seem to "work" are the ones that are simple but still unique enough to not be boring. I continually try to challenge myself by working on new projects, especially projects that involve coding, to keep my skills sharp.

Why choose me? Well, I have a strong eye for detail, over 7 years of graphic design experience, over 2 years of web design experience (this includes coding with HTML, CSS, & JavaScript), and I learn very quickly. I bring a unique perspective to every project because of my vast array of skills that I bring to the table.
